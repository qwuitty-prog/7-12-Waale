How It Works

Your Plot in 4 Simple Steps

A clear, transparent process so you always know exactly where you stand and what comes next.

01

Share Your Requirement

Tell us your budget, location, and purpose — residential, agricultural, or investment. We shortlist the best-matched plots for you.

02

Site Visit & 7/12 Review

We arrange guided site visits and walk you through 7/12, 8A, and village map extracts in plain language — no jargon.

03

Legal Due Diligence

Our legal team verifies title chain, encumbrances, mutations, and NOCs. You proceed only when everything is completely clean.
