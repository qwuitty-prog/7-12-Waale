The Big Picture

What Is Mumbai 3.0?

Mumbai 3.0 is an upcoming planned urban region in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR), being developed under the Government of Maharashtra and MMRDA. It is strategically located between Mumbai and Navi Mumbai, spread mainly across the Uran, Panvel, and Pen belt, covering approximately 124 villages.

With major infrastructure projects like the Navi Mumbai International Airport (NMIA), the Atal Setu sea bridge, and upcoming connectivity corridors, this region is set to become the next growth hub of the MMR — the way Navi Mumbai once evolved from planned concept to thriving city.

Mumbai 3.0 represents the future expansion of Mumbai — offering better planning, improved connectivity, and strong investment potential. At 7/12 Waale, we offer premium, investment-friendly plots right inside this belt, helping you build a secure and growing real estate portfolio.
